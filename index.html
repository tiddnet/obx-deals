OBX Deals

Vacation rental price intelligence for the Outer Banks of North Carolina.

What we track

We monitor weekly rates, calendar availability, and listing visibility across the major property management companies serving the Outer Banks — currently around five thousand vacation rental properties across sixteen PMCs, refreshed nightly.

What we publish

  • Price-drop notifications for travelers tracking specific weeks or markets.
  • Comparative market analytics so owners and travelers can see where a given week sits relative to comparable inventory.
  • Private Owner Reports for property owners who want a longitudinal view of how their home is priced versus the market.

How we use email

Email is used for two narrow transactional purposes: price-drop and market-alert notifications that subscribers opt into by request, and 1–1 Owner Reports delivered to property owners who have asked for them by direct contact. No marketing lists, no bulk sends, no purchased addresses.

OBX Deals is an independent project. Property listings and rates are sourced from publicly accessible PMC websites; we are not affiliated with any property management company.
