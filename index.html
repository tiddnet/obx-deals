Our story

We own two vacation rentals on the Outer Banks — a soundfront cottage in Avon and a poolside house in Corolla — and for years we struggled with the same question every OBX owner faces: how do I know if my rates are right?

The tools didn't exist. PMC market reports are opaque. Comparing your calendar to a competitor meant opening dozens of browser tabs and doing rough math in a spreadsheet. We had no reliable way to see where we sat in the market, which weeks were underpriced, or whether our property management company was doing us any favors.

So we built one. What started as a script to track a handful of competitors grew into a nightly intelligence system that monitors rates, availability, and visibility across 9,000+ vacation rentals on the Outer Banks — 25 property management companies, refreshed every morning before sunrise.

The search engine and deal alerts are the public face of that system. The owner tools are what we actually run our rentals on.

Privacy

When you click an outbound listing link we record the destination, an anonymous browser ID (a UUID stored as a first-party cookie, no email or identifier ever attached), the country your request came from, and a coarse device class. We do not store IP addresses, full user-agent strings, or any third-party identifier. Records expire 365 days after the click. To opt out: enable Do Not Track in your browser, block track.obx.deals in an ad blocker, or clear the obxd_vid cookie — the site works either way.
